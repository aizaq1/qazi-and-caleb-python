Write each program using concatenation (+ signs) and also using formatted strings(with f).

1. Can you make a program that asks the person their name? The program will then say their name back in a nice way. For example:

Enter your name: Qazi
Hi, Qazi! How are you?

2. Can you make a program that asks the person their favorite hobby? The program will then say their hobby back in a nice way. For example:

Enter your favorite hobby: biking
Nice! I like biking too!
